Supported Technical Indicators

Bollinger Bands (BB)

Momentum indicator that allow users to observe the price strength and volatility to generate oversold and overbought indicators.

Exponential Moving Average (EMA)

Price trends smoothed out over n-period. Combination of a long and short period interactions indicates trend changes.

Relative Strength Index (RSI)

Momentum oscillator used by traders to measure the speed and magnitude of price movements to generate oversold and overbought indicators.

Stochastic RSI (SRSI)

Based on the RSI value itself rather than the asset price. SRSI reacts much faster to generate oversold and overbought indicators compared to RSI.
